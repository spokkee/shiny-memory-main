Question 1: _____ fees are capitalised.

  1. Revenue
  2. Membership
  3. Capital Fund
  4. None of the above
  
Membership

Question 2: The fixed value of a share is called _____ value of a share.

  1. face value
  2. deducted
  3. uncalled capital
  4. All of the above
  
face value

Question 3: _____ expenditure is non-recurring.

  1. Capital
  2. D.R. Expenses
  3. Revenue
  4. None of the above
  
Capital

Question 4: Super profit is

  1. Excess of future maintainable profit over normal profit.
  2. Average profit.
  3. Normal profit.
  4. Abnormal profit.
  
Excess of future maintainable profit over normal profit.

  3. Trial Balance
  4. All of the above
