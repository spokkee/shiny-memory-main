Question 31 : What is the territorial jurisdiction of the Patents Act, 1970?

  1. It extends to whole of India except for the State of Jammu & Kashmir
  2. It extends to whole of India
  3. It extends to whole of India except for the Union Territories and State of Jammu & Kashmir.
  4. It extends to whole of India and members of the World Intellectual Property Organization
  
It extends to whole of India

Question 32 : ...................., in relation to an invention, means that the invention is capable of being made or used in an industry

  1. patent
  2. capable of industrial application
  3. exclusive licence
  4. international applicant
  
capable of industrial application

Question 33 : Which Form can be used for requesting early publication of a patent?

  1. Form 2
  2. Form 1 and 2
  3. Form 9
  4. Form 5
  
Form 1 and 2

Question 34 : What is the concerns regarding genomics?

Question 36 : What are types of patents in Australian Scenario?

Question 37 : Patent rights with respect to any invention are created only upon___ of the patent by the Patent Office following the procedure established by the Patents Act and the Rules

  1. grant
  2. delivery
  3. licence
  4. establishment

Question 38 : The ____ builds on the interest of society in works of intellectual property, holding that legal protection for intellectual works serves as an incentive to produce more intellectual works that will ultimately benefit society

  1. Reward theory
  2. Incentive theory
  3. Prospect theory
  4. Economic theory
  
Incentive theory

Question 39 : Patent application contains_____

  1. Form-1
  2. Form-2
  3. Form-1 and Form-2
  4. Form-30

Question 40 : Indian Patent system has

  1. Pre-grant opposition only
  2. Post-grant opposition only
  3. both pre-grant and post-grant opposition
  4. process opposition only
  
both pre-grant and post-grant opposition

Question 41 : A request for expedited examination can be filed in the following cases

  1. If the applicant is a Startup
  2. If the applicant is a Natural Person
  3. In an international application, India has been elected as an International Preliminary Examining Authority
  4. both A and B

Question 42 : Intellectual property law does incentivise .......

  1. protection
  2. security

  4. rights

Question 43 : National patents are available in which European countries

  1. all European countries

Question 44 : Intellectual property rights are _____ over the use of his/her creation for a certain period of time.

  1. excessive rights

Question 45 : Which of the following are remedies to breach of confidential information?

  1. infringement

Question 46 : An inventor was awarded a patent in U.K. on a method for selecting transformed plants and has practiced the mentioned method only in U.K. Six months later, another person who independently invented the same method in Australia wants to obtain a patent in Australia. She:

  1. could do it without major problems
  2. would not be able to do it because the granted patent was published in the U.K.
  3. would not be able to do it because the method is used in the U.K.
  4. would not be able to do it because the method is used in the U.K and patent granted was published in UK
  
would not be able to do it because the granted patent was published in the U.K.

Question 54 : A policy (or policies) behind the patent system is:

  1. to encourage an inventor to disclose an invention by granting exclusive rights over the invention to the inventor.
  2. to not benefit the public by limiting the scope and term of the exclusive rights granted to an inventor.
