Question 1 : Where is the headquarters of USPTO located?

  1. Detroit, Michigan
  2. Denver, Colorado
  3. Dallas, Texas
  4. Alexandria, Virginia
  
Alexandria, Virginia

Question 2 : Indian National IPR Policy came out in the year

  1. 2016
  2. 2000
  3. 2001
  4. 1970
  
2016

Question 3 : Patil Jewellers is a bad trademark. Reason is.......

  1. Common Names, Names of community and name of person should be avoided
  2. Jewellery business must not have a trademark
  3. Nature of business should not be a part of trademark
  4. It is already taken by someone
  
Common Names, Names of community and name of person should be avoided

Question 4 : Which of the following is not a category of copyright work

  1. Literary works
  2. Furniture
  3. Sculpture
  4. Musical work
  
Furniture

Question 5 : Patent can be infringed

  1. Directly only
  2. In-directly only
  3. directly as well as indirectly
  4. Exclusively
  
directly as well as indirectly

Question 6 : Duration of patent is

  1. 20 years from the date of application
  2. 20 years from the date of publication
  3. 20 years from the date of grant
  4. 20 years from the date of examination
  
20 years from the date of application

Question 7 : An application for a patent shall ordinarily be published after

  1. 12 months
  2. 18 months
  3. 24 months
  4. 36 months
  
18 months

Question 8 : Which court has the jurisdiction to entertain a trademark infringement suit?

  1. High Court
  2. special court
  3. supreme court
  4. labour court
  
High Court

Question 9 : Defensive protection in traditional knowledge aims to _______________

  1. encourage people outside the community from acquiring intellectual property rights over traditional knowledge
  2. stop people outside the community from acquiring intellectual property rights over traditional knowledge
  3. Hide IP
  4. stop people withinthe community from acquiring intellectual property rights over traditional knowledge
  
stop people outside the community from acquiring intellectual property rights over traditional knowledge

Question 10 : The publication of a patent application shall be available

  1. Official Journal of the Patent office
  2. Official Gazette of the Government of India
  3. Website of the Press Information Bureau
  4. National Newspapers on every Tuesday
  
Official Journal of the Patent office

Question 11 : A party may seek a compulsory license, after _______of the grant of a patent.

  1. 2 years
  2. 3 years
  3. 4 years
  4. 5 years
  
3 years

Question 12 : Upon obtaining a provisional patent, the inventor/inventors may use _____ tag when they try to find the commercial potential of their new design/process.

  1. Patent completed
  2. Patent resume
  3. Patent pending
  4. Patent submitted
  
Patent pending

Question 13 : Discovery of a new galaxy is

  1. a patentable subject matter
  2. non-patentable under section 3(a) of Indian Patent Act 1970
  3. non-patentable under section 3(b) of Indian Patent Act 1970
  4. non-patentable under section 3(c) of Indian Patent Act 1970
  
non-patentable under section 3(c) of Indian Patent Act 1970

Question 14 : Biological Diversity means ___________

  1. Biological Destruction
  2. the similiarity among living organisms from all sources and the ecological complexes
  3. the variability among living organisms from all sources and the ecological complexes
  4. Biological Disturbance
  
the variability among living organisms from all sources and the ecological complexes

Question 15 : How many types of compulsory licenses are provided for under the Indian patent Act?

  1. 1
  2. 2
  3. 3
  4. 4
  
4

Question 16 : A patent protects the .......... aspects of a product.

  1. design
  2. commercial
  3. non-functional
  4. functional
  
functional

Question 17 : what is the problem for the incumbent manufacturers in many industries?

  1. they may not own the intellectual property (IP) that new digital technologies are built upon
  2. they may own the intellectual property (IP) that new digital technologies are built upon
  3. world is changing slow
  4. Internet

Question 18 : Local and indigenous communities are using traditional knowledge for ______

  1. Agriculture and food storage
  2. sturucture
  3. construction
  4. destruction

Question 19 : As per____ a work created by an individual represents their specific character as an individual, natural rights demands that we accept the resulting development as its creator's exclusive property.

  1. Personality Theory
  2. Labour Theory
  3. Natural theory
  4. Social Contract Theory

Question 20 : What is genomics?

  1. Genomics is the study of the only part of the genetic or epigenetic sequence information of organisms
  2. Genomics is the study of the economics and genetis
  3. Genomics is the study of information
  4. Genomics is the study of knowledge
  
Genomics is the study of the only part of the genetic or epigenetic sequence information of organisms

Question 21 : WIPO is situated at..

  1. London
  2. New York
  3. Geneva
  4. Doha

Question 22 : Patent is not granted

  1. new technical solution to a problem
  2. process
  3. product
  4. for an idea or principle

Question 23 : Design patents may be granted to whom in US Scenario?

  1. anyone who uses design
  2. anyone who replicates useful process or machine
  3. anyone who invents a new, original, and ornamental design for an article of manufacture
  4. anyone who replicates a ornamental design

Question 24 : Opposition Board consists of

  1. 4 members
  2. 3 members
  3. 5 members
  4. 7 members

Question 25 : A patent is ____________granted by a country to the owner of an invention to make, use, manufacture and market the invention, provided the invention satisfies certain conditions stipulated in the law.

  1. exclusive right
  2. Inclusive right
  3. Both a and b
  4. tangible property

Question 26 : Is Indian Patent Act 1970 applicable to Ladakh Union Territory?
